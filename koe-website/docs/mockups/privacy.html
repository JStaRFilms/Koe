Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. We don't collect anything.

Koe is completely local. Voice Activity Detection (VAD) happens exclusively on your machine. We do not track you, we do not log your activity, and we have no central database containing your transcriptions.

2. API Key Storage

Your Groq API key is stored locally on your machine in encrypted state via the standard OS credential manager. It is only brought into memory precisely when needed to authenticate transcription requests to Groq servers.

3. Audio Processing

Your audio is only sent to Groq for transcription when you hold down your designated dictation shortcut. Please refer to Groq's Privacy Policy regarding how they handle uploaded audio chunks during transcription.

4. Open Source Transparency

Don't take our word for it. Koe is built entirely in the open. You can verify all of these claims by reading the source code yourself on GitHub.

Have questions?

The best place to reach us regarding privacy or security concerns is via GitHub Issues.
